Mystery of making soup

A bowl of soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 0.25 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 0.25 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives

Steps

  1. Stir together cornstarch and water to dissolve cornstarch; pour into the boiling broth. Stir gently while you pour in egg and yellow food coloring. Season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle with chives before serving.
